what is openactive?

Openactive represents a group of organisations who share a vision for tackling the global problem of physical inactivity. We use technology to help more people to become more active, by providing open source tools, open standards and open data. We are uniting those working in the sector under the common goal of making physical activity data (e.g. bookable squash courts, running groups, and yoga classes) openly available for anyone to freely access, use and share. We are creating the data infrastructure that will enable data sharing and creating standards to facilitate innovation.

Our mission is to work together to solve the problems that arise when trying to connect multiple systems in the complex health and fitness ecosystem. We aim to discuss and prepare data and open API specifications, validators, and related open source components, as well as open supporting materials, which facilitate the use of open and shared data relating to sport and physical activity. This is a grassroots movement encouraging open innovation through collaboration, and Openactive.io is a place to demonstrate all we can achieve when we work together.

Pioneers of Openactive are, where applicable, (i) committed to facilitating providers in making their sessions and facilities openly shared and openly bookable, such that third parties can easily incorporate their physical activity data in innovative digital products, (ii) committed to using open physical activity data to get their audience more active, and (iii) committed to spreading the message of Openactive within the sector.

If you think your organisation can help support the growing ecosystem, why not join us to help shape the future of your sector?
