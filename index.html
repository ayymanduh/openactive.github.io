what is openactive?

Openactive represents a group of organisations who share a vision for tackling the global problem of physical inactivity.

Our mission is to solve the problems that arise when trying to connect multiple systems and companies in the complex health and fitness ecosystem. Uniting those working in the sector under a common goal, we are creating the data infrastructure that will enable data sharing and create standards to facilitate innovation. This is a grassroots movement encouraging innovation through collaboration, and Openactive.io is a place to demonstrate all we can achieve when we work together.

If you think your organisation can help support the growing ecosystem, why not join us to help shape the future of your sector?
