LICENSING AND CERTIFICATION

The Open Data Institute recommends the Creative Commons Attribution License (cc-by) version 4 for data published by the Openactive community. CC-BY is the most common license in use for publishing open data, and importantly version 4 includes specific provision for database rights. Generic open data licensing guidance is also available.

The Open Data Institute also encourage all publishers to create an Open Data Certificate for all open data published.
