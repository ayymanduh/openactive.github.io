SHARE THE MESSAGE

The Openactive message can be freely and easily shared by anyone, whether they are a Pioneer or not. The content here is all openly licensed, so you are free to share, edit and re-use it in any way you see fit (just make sure you keep the OPENACTIVE.IO attribution intact). The materials below are designed to help those who see the benefits of open data in our sector spread the word and work of the community and move our sector forward.
